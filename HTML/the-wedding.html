The Wedding

Being the practical people we are, we decided to get married on the same day as our dating anniversary. That way we didn't have to learn a new date. Can't get much simpler than April 1st, right?

Disney offered so many choices for weddings. After serious consideration and lots of research on Jessie's part we chose an intimate ceremony to be held at The Grand Floridian. Perfect because it's Joe's favorite resort on Disney property and we had our first magical Disney experience there.

Intimate truly does mean small. We'll be joined by only 4 other people for the ceremony. It was tough to choose because we love every one of our family and friends. A few other family members and friends who are able to make the trip to Florida will join us for some fun times in the parks and all sorts food adventures. We'll be in Disney World in the middle of the Flower and Garden Festival that happens every spring.
