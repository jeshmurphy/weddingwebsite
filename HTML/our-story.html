Our Story

Sometimes things work out in the strangest ways. Follow our story in the timeline below.

2010

Jes and Joe met during the fall semester at Montclair State University. She would often invite him out for coffee after class, but he always declined. They went about their lives not really thinking of the other.

2011

Early in the spring semester Joe decided to finally go out for coffee. At this point Jes had moved on and was busy with other interests, but she agreed anyway. They spent 10 hours talking at a local diner, Joe was making up for all the declined coffee dates. He was smitten. She was oblivious.

Over the next couple of months Joe found a way to see more and more of Jes. He gave her a gift of a book, IQ, one of their shared favorites. Joe REALLY won her over when he had a custom cake made in her favorite flavor - key lime.

They made it official April 1st.

2012

Joe braved his allergy to cats for love. So he packed up his belongings and ventured to New York City with Jes and her cat, Neko. The pair fulfilled their lifelong dream of living in New York.

2016
